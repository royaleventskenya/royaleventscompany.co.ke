5 Event Trends in Kenya You Need to Know in 2025

The events industry in Kenya is evolving rapidly. From technology to sustainability, 2025 promises exciting new possibilities. Here are the top 5 event trends you should know:

1. Eco-Friendly Events

More couples and companies are choosing green options—like reusable décor, digital invitations, and waste management plans.

2. AI-Powered Event Planning

Artificial intelligence is helping planners predict guest preferences, personalize experiences, and automate logistics.

3. Hybrid & Virtual Add-ons

Even physical events now include live streams for remote guests, expanding reach and inclusivity.

4. Immersive Guest Experiences

From 360-degree photo booths to interactive entertainment, guests now expect more engagement than ever before.

5. Bold, Local Themes

African-inspired designs, Kenyan cuisines, and cultural performances are becoming staples for creating authentic experiences.
